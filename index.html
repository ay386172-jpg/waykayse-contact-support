Before you write

Many issues have quick fixes — try these first:

  • Alarm not ringing? Open iOS Settings → Notifications → Wakly and make sure alerts, sounds, and badges are all allowed.
  • Missed alarm in silent mode? Wakly can bypass silent mode only on devices where Critical Alerts have been approved.
  • Mission not detecting you? Check lighting and make sure the camera lens is clean.
  • Purchase issue? Settings → Premium → Restore Purchases usually resolves it.
